Our Mission

Defenders of Wildlife, founded in 1947, is a leading U.S. conservation organization dedicated to safeguarding native animals and plants and their habitats. They advocate for policy reforms, employ scientific expertise, and engage in wildlife management to protect imperiled species across North America. With a multi-pronged approach, they influence decision-making in various arenas, including Congress, courts, and public discourse. Their field offices enable community engagement and practical conservation solutions. Supported by members and partners, Defenders of Wildlife strives to be a potent and enduring force for wildlife conservation, employing knowledge, advocacy, and public support to ensure the preservation of natural ecosystems.

Our Conservation Vision

Defenders of Wildlife envisions secure and thriving wildlife populations across North America, supported by healthy lands and waters. They prioritize protecting a diverse range of species, including migratory ones, and recognize the interdependence of species and habitats. Their goal is to ensure vulnerable species rebound and flourish in well-distributed populations, free from the threat of rapid decline or extinction. Achieving this vision requires a transnational network of both public and private lands, rivers, coastal waters, and working landscapes. Through targeted conservation efforts, Defenders of Wildlife aims to create a sustainable future where wildlife and ecosystems thrive harmoniously.

Our Strategic Plan

Defenders of Wildlife's strategic plan outlines objectives and resource allocation to realize their vision effectively. Emphasizing long-term solutions with measurable outcomes, it aims to bolster the organization's role as a potent advocate for wildlife conservation. By clearly defining their vision and concentrating resources, they aim to safeguard North America's diverse wildlife and habitats for future generations.

Our Values

Defenders of Wildlife operates under a belief in the intrinsic value of wildlife and the natural world, valuing all species and habitats regardless of their utility to humans. Guided by a comprehensive values statement, they prioritize integrity, collaboration, and inclusivity in all endeavors. Committed to unity as "One Defenders," they emphasize diversity and collaboration in advancing wildlife conservation goals. Using the best available science and technology while remaining pragmatic, they strive for lasting solutions consistent with their mission. Mindful resource management ensures maximum impact in advocacy efforts while minimizing environmental impact. Defenders fosters a fair, equitable workplace, prioritizing employee welfare and professional growth. They embrace empowerment, accountability, and diversity, recognizing their collective strength in working towards a shared goal of preserving the planet and its inhabitants.

Diversity

Defenders of Wildlife is dedicated to advancing wildlife conservation amidst the pressing biodiversity and climate crises, emphasizing collaboration and inclusivity. They prioritize creating a positive, inclusive workplace where every voice is valued, recognizing the vital role of engaged staff in fulfilling their conservation mission. The organization celebrates the expertise and dedication of its employees in tackling challenges such as biodiversity loss and climate change. Additionally, Defenders acknowledges Indigenous Peoples as the original stewards of North America's land, waters, and wildlife, committing to respectful collaboration and the incorporation of traditional ecological knowledge. They strive to build equitable partnerships with historically marginalized communities, valuing and integrating Indigenous guidance and priorities into their conservation efforts nationwide.
